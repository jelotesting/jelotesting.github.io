How it works

1. CREATE AN ACCOUNT

To get Started, you have to create an account.

Enter the amount you wish to purchase a giftcard, after a prompt will be sent to your mobile phone where you will enter your PIN to approve transaction.

To access the GiftCard, the person receiving the gift card has to claim it by tapping on the 'CLAIM GIFTCARD' on his link webpage. That's all!!
